## Specifications

- A switch must have a visible label that clearly conveys what option a user will turn on or off.

### Behaviors

- Avoid changing the label text based on on and off state.

### Editorial

- Avoid acronyms.
- Use 2-5 words.
- Use the same label text for on and off states
